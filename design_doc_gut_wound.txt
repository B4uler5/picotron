Gutwound

Story Driven Survival Game

Core gameplay
Exploration of Tower while managing many ailments
Deal with gut wound by:
	- stitching wound
	- applying antiseptic
	- taking pain killers
	- taking antibiotics
	- drinking lots of water
	- moving less.
Collect items to heal by searching rooms/storage
Find interesting old world items like:
	- wind up grammerphone with records
	- vcr attached to back up generator
	- etc
	
Design Challenges:
	- Why is it fun?
	- How long should it be?
	- What's the bloody story?
